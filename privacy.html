Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Cumulus ("we", "us") runs a London community events platform. This page explains what information we collect, why, and the choices you have. We're a small team — if anything here is unclear, email hello@cumulusapp.co and we'll help directly.

What we collect

  • Account details — name and email address when you sign up or log in.
  • Profile content — anything you choose to add to your Cumulus Pass: bio, interests, photo, badges.
  • Event activity — RSVPs, tickets, check-ins, and events you host or apply to host.
  • Location — only when you use map or check-in features, to show nearby events and confirm you're at a venue. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Messages — messages you send to other members through in-app chat.
  • Curator / referral codes — used to verify perk access.
  • Host information — for anyone applying to host events: venue or business name, event details, and category.

How we use it

To run the core service: show you relevant events, manage bookings and tickets, connect attendees before and during events, verify perk unlocks, and review event submissions before they go live. We don't sell your data, and we don't run advertising trackers.

Payments

Ticket payments and host payouts are processed by Stripe. Cumulus never sees or stores your full card details. For hosted events, ticket funds are held in escrow and released to the host's connected Stripe account after the event, per the terms shown at checkout.

Who else sees data

We use a small number of service providers to run Cumulus, each of whom processes only what they need to do their job:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider.
  • Mapbox — powers maps and location lookups.
  • Stripe — payment processing and host payouts.
  • Google Fonts — web fonts used across the site.

On your device

Cumulus stores a small amount of data locally in your browser — your login session, theme preference, and cached map lookups — purely to make the app faster. This never leaves your device and isn't shared with us beyond what's already covered above.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time — email hello@cumulusapp.co. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you think we've mishandled your data.

Children

Cumulus is not directed at children under 16, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
